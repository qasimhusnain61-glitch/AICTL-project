A Culinary Journey

Located in the heart of tradition, Haveli Heritage brings you recipes passed down through generations. From slow-cooked curries to hand-crafted breads, every bite tells a story of our rich culture.

Chef's Signature Specials

Karachi Beef Nihari

A slow-cooked beef stew from the heart of Karachi, simmered overnight with bone marrow and a blend of 32 aromatic spices.

Heritage BBQ Platter

A grand selection of charcoal-grilled Seekh Kebabs, Malai Tikka, and lamb chops, served with mint chutney and hot naan.

Lahori Tawa Fish

Fresh river fish marinated in crushed pomegranate seeds and gram flour, shallow-fried on a traditional iron tawa.
