Our Story & Philosophy

Preserving the Art of Traditional Hospitality

The Legacy of the Haveli

Our journey began in 1985, when our founder decided to create a space where the "slow-cooking" methods of his ancestors could be shared with the world. He believed that modern kitchens had lost the 'soul' of food by rushing the process.

The term "Haveli" signifies a sanctuary of hospitality. Every spice we use is hand-ground, and every recipe is a guarded secret passed down through three generations of master chefs.

Our Journey Through Time

The Farm to Haveli Initiative

We believe great food starts in the soil. 80% of our vegetables are sourced directly from our own organic farm, ensuring that ingredients are harvested the very morning they are served to you.

Hand-Ground Spices

We avoid factory powders. Every morning, our spice masters use traditional stone grinders to crush fresh turmeric and chilies, preserving the essential oils and soul of the dish.
